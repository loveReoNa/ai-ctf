Done:     Compared three memory approaches:
          [1] Buffer Memory (current approach): retains the most recent N
              conversation turns; simple but token consumption grows linearly.
          [2] Summary Memory: uses an LLM to periodically compress history into
              a summary; reduces tokens but may lose details.

          Buffer + Summary combined; long-term (across challenges) uses Vector
          Store to persist key findings.

          wastes tokens; too infrequent degrades summary quality.

          when the Buffer exceeds 20 messages; retain the 5 most recent raw
